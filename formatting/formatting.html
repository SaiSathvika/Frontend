Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in modern cricket. Known for his aggressive playing style, consistency, and leadership skills, Kohli has achieved numerous records and accolades throughout his career.Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India. He was introduced to cricketat a young age and quickly rose through the ranks of domestic cricket. He gained national attention when he captained the Indian Under-19 team to victory in the 2008 ICC Under-19 Cricket World Cup.Kohli's impact on Indian cricket is immeasurable, and his influence continues to grow, both as a

player and as a role model
for aspiring cricketers.
